About the college

Built on a founder's word, run on a discipline that hasn't changed.

Angappa College of Arts and Science was established in 1987 by the Angappa Educational Trust, founded by Dr. T.V. Angappan — an educationist who spent his career building institutions across Coimbatore in service of accessible education. The college has stayed affiliated to Bharathiar University since its founding.

The campus sits in a quiet, green setting on the outskirts of the city — space the college treats as part of the education itself, alongside a standing insistence on punctuality, regularity and academic rigour.

A resident faculty team runs undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across Commerce, Computer Science and Management, with placement support built into the final years of every course.
